Our approach

Ontogeny: capability that matures through use.

We believe AI systems should evolve like organisms: sensing context, adapting to feedback, and strengthening as they interact with real work. Every engagement is designed to move from discovery to durable capability.

  1. Discover

    Clarify the decision, workflow, or product outcome worth augmenting.

  2. Prototype

    Build a thin working slice so teams can test behavior instead of debating abstractions.

  3. Instrument

    Add evaluation, observability, data stewardship, and human review where it matters.

  4. Operationalize

    Document the system, train the team, and create a roadmap for the next generation.
